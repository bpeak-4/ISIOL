The Institute for the Study of the Indian Ocean Littoral

About Us

The Institute for the Study of the Indian Ocean Littoral undertakes, promotes, and supports scholarship on the Indian Ocean region across institutional and disciplinary divides. In so doing, it affirms a multiplicity of coeval and correlated interconnected histories across times and spaces.

Research

Our research initiatives foster collaborative and interdisciplinary engagements that explore the diverse cultures, histories, ecologies, and economies that shape and are shaped by the Indian Ocean littoral.
